About Me

I am a Level 5 student in Electrical and Electronics Communication Engineering with a strong passion for Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) in cybersecurity. My engineering background provides me with a unique technical foundation to understand complex systems and their security requirements.

My interest in GRC began when I recognized the critical need for structured security frameworks in increasingly connected technological systems. I believe that effective governance is essential for ensuring that security measures align with business objectives while managing risks appropriately.

I have developed expertise in various cybersecurity frameworks including ISO 27001, NIST CSF, and SOC2, and have practical experience in risk assessment, policy development, and compliance auditing through academic projects and self-study.

My goal is to become a GRC specialist who can help organizations build resilient security postures that are both compliant with regulations and aligned with their business goals.
